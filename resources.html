Here, you can find links to reputable organizations for research, financial aid, counseling services, and community support groups. This section is provided as a community service and is not affiliated with any of the listed organizations, nor does it receive any financial compensation from them. Our goal is to help you access the support and information you need in one convenient place.

American Cancer Society

Dedicated to helping persons who face cancer. Supports research, patient services, early detection, treatment and education.

Cancer Support Community

Offers a network of services and education for patients and their families.

National Cancer Institute

Part of the U.S. National Institutes of Health, it conducts and supports research, training, health information dissemination.

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital

Advancing cures, and means of prevention, for pediatric catastrophic diseases through research and treatment.

Susan G. Komen for the Cure

Focused on breast cancer education, research, advocacy, health services and social support programs in the U.S.

Leukemia & Lymphoma Society

Dedicated to fighting blood cancers, providing patient support and advocacy.

Dana-Farber Cancer Institute

Committed to providing the best treatment available today while developing tomorrow's cures through cutting-edge research.

Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center

One of the world's premier cancer centers, dedicated to exceptional patient care, leading-edge research, and superb educational programs.

Stand Up To Cancer

Innovative research approach to accelerate the development of new cancer treatments.

About

Brave Battlers is dedicated to raising cancer awareness and supporting those affected by this challenging disease. Our site offers educational resources, personal stories of resilience, and the latest developments in cancer treatment and prevention.
